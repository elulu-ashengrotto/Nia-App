              Backstory .  

The story so far...    \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \

Nia grew up on the island of Twawaii, a sheltered paradise filled with ocean, sun, sand, waves, anything the population could ask for. With both her parents being born and bred locals, from a young age she was taught to swim, surf and dive; all manner of water activities that gave her a love for the deep blue waves surrounding her home.

Her mother was a champion of the surfing arena, and Nia looked up to her more than anything. On the other hand, her father taught her the basics of island life, teaching her how to cook, fish, and various other activities. Her upbringing was filled with happy memories, and soon enough Nia gained several siblings to spend her time with. The set of them were inseparable; two sisters and a brother, and Nia herself.

When Nia was 16, her mother and father died in a surfing accident, in which they were trapped in a tropical storm, leaving her the sole benefactor and protector of her siblings. She tried her hardest to provide them a good life, often going without so that her own siblings would be fed and clothed. Her social life suffered as a result, as she would often work long hours as a waitress to provide for them. This also resulted in a slight loneliness within her, something she struggles with to this day. Nia's magical powers manifested as a direct result of working overtime and caring for her siblings, always wishing she could do more, always needing to be one step ahead. Eventually, she grew proficient enough to complete chores, moving on to cooking meals, then going to develop her potent Unique Magic during a tough time in the waves

During a certain someone’s vacation in Twawaii, Nia and her now girlfriend Rune start to become close.

After receiving her letter to Royal Sword Academy, her siblings and neighbours encouraged her to attend- despite Nia insisting she stay to take care of her family. The island rallied around the family to support them, and Nia eventually accepted, making her way to the magical school.

     Appearance .  

A girl of fairly average stature, standing at 5’4”. Too used to wearing sandals, she refrains from wearing heels to increase her height, always choosing comfort over fashion—it was often unlikely she had spare cash to treat herself to new clothes while she was looking after her sister. Nia sports a slightly curvy build, with slender legs and soft shoulders. Her face is also on the softer side, kind, ocean-blue eyes complementing her deep-brown hair. Whether or not she wears makeup depends on her energy levels for the day.

Most of the time, she can be seen sporting casual clothing: tank tops, shorts, sandals—perhaps even a cozy sweater should the weather at RSA require it. Her ears are pierced, preferring small studs or teardrop earrings that keep out of the way during surfing. Despite her inability to afford fashionable items, Nia takes great joy in creating outfits, styling others, and window shopping.

     Personality .  

Nia is an incredibly bubbly girl, outgoing and outspoken, never afraid to use her voice to speak out against wrongdoings or injustices. Her motherly nature has only grown since having her sister in her care, and thus she often seems mature for her age. It’s not uncommon to see her cleaning up after others, making sure her fellow dorm mates have eaten, and double—no, triple—checking every detail. Despite her nurturing composure when it comes to others, Nia often neglects her own needs, resulting in an unkempt room or perhaps even the odd nap during lunchtime due to a lack of sleep.

When it comes to romance, Nia is the opposite of composed. The slightest ounce of attention or praise is a surefire way to have her blushing deeper than the ocean trenches around Twawaii, something her girlfriend Rune often takes advantage of. Although she loves her dearly, the long distance takes its toll, and Nia often feels a sense of loneliness without her.

              Unique Magic                      

Gravitas Shift !    \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \

 

    Gravitas Shift!          When used, Nia’s unique magic allows her to manipulate the gravity around her. The exact radius is determined by how much magic she chooses to use, and the gravity will be changed for as long as she can hold the spell active. Nia can increase or decrease the gravity, making this a potentially dangerous magic that Nia herself must keep in check.

Often, Nia will use her unique magic to grant herself more airtime while surfing or to rescue bowls and plates her siblings may accidentally drop. Her unique magic is activated via touch, meaning she simply needs to make physical contact with the object or area to be affected.

 

              Roleplay Sample .  

In which Nia considers the distance between herself and Rune    \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \

Nia sighs as she rolls over in her dorm bed. The storm cloud over her head doesn’t seem to cease today, casting its gloomy weather over her demeanor. Most of the time, she wouldn’t miss club for the world, but today seems stacked against her, the world throwing every hardship at her. She’s glad she chose to skip, just for this week. Her loneliness hadn’t been this bad in quite a while. For some time, actually. Not since she’d met Rune. Their first week, where they had been utterly swamped with exams and classwork, too busy to text, too busy to call, and Nia couldn’t take it. Just perfect.

The bangle on her wrist glints in the dim light of Auraspire’s dorm room, the rays catching the engraving on the silver. At least its weight is a good reminder, but Nia can’t help but crave her touch, the sound of her laughter, her voice; the kisses, the cuddles, the handholding—just everything! Ugh-

The brunette slams her head into her pillow, groaning as she does so. She was supposed to be more put together than this, damn it! Except clearly, she signed up to this fate from the very moment she met Rune on that beach; the moment she took her hand so willingly, showed her how to surf, how to dive, the local delicacies, the bars.

In return, Rune had shown her how to kiss, how to love, how to hug, and how to cherish another in a way she almost thought she’d never experience. The soft blush on her cheeks intensified to a deep red as her thoughts wandered, desperate to be strolling the beach again—hand in hand, of course.

As fate would have it, there’s a sudden ping from her phone, which lies strewn on her messy desk. Nia nearly launches herself across the room to scramble for it, reading the message with a lovestruck expression. It’s from Rune!

‘Hi bb. I missed you…’

Nia’s well manicured hands quickly type out something in reply.

’I missed you too. Call?’
